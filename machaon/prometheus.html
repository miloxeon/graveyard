If you have an iPhone, look at its icons and their corners.
As you can see, variable content sizes don't skew the curve proportions in the slightest.
It wouldn't have been the case if we have just set an SVG background-image.
It's also not CSS paint API. No JS is involved.
It works just like border-radius.
